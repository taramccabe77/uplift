How to export from Lose It

  1. Go to loseit.com and sign in
  2. Click Insights at the top, then Weekly Summary on the left
  3. Use the arrows to select your week, then click Export
  4. Repeat for each week you want to import — then upload them all at once here

Garmin Connect
⏰ Time for your weekly Garmin sync — it's been 7 days since your last sync.

Sync heart rate, stress, sleep, and steps directly — no file needed. Requires the sync server running on your PC.

The sync button works when uplift_garmin_sync.py is running on your PC. Download it and follow the setup instructions inside.

Download sync server script

Or upload a UDSFile manually

  1. Go to connect.garmin.com → profile icon → Account Settings
  2. Data Management → Export Your Data → request export
  3. Garmin emails you a zip — unzip and open DI_CONNECT / DI-Connect-User
  4. Upload the file starting with UDSFile_ for HR & stress, or the file starting with SleepData_ for sleep duration & quality
